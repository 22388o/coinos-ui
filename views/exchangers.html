Meeting Procedure

  • Contact one of us to let us know your availability and how much you want to spend
  • We'll arrange a time to meet at a public place like a café or a bank
  • At the time of meeting, we'll look up the exchange rate above to determine how many bitcoins to send for the amount you specified
  • You'll provide us with a bitcoin address to send to and count out the cash for us
  • We'll send the bitcoins to your address using a phone or laptop
  • When the transaction is confirmed, we take the cash and you keep the bitcoins!
