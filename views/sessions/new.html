Point of Sale

Use this page to receive and track bitcoin payments

Accounts are free to register and we don't charge any transaction fees

Login with demo/demo to try it out


How It Works

You specify a bitcoin address payments should be sent to

Our page allows you to create payment requests in your local currency

The amount is converted into bitcoins according to the exchange rate of your choosing

Your customers scan a QR code to get your address and the amount in bitcoins

The page detects when the correct payment amount is received to your address and displays a notification

You can view a history of all payments received and the exchange rate used at the time of sale
